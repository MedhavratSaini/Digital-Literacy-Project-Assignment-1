Following is a checklist of things that one must do and not do on social media:-
✅ Do’s
Think before you post – Ensure your content is respectful and appropriate.
Protect your privacy – Adjust privacy settings and avoid sharing personal information.
Be respectful and positive – Engage in constructive conversations and avoid online conflicts.
Verify information before sharing – Check facts to prevent spreading misinformation.
Maintain a professional image – Remember that future employers may view your profile.
❌ Don’ts
Don’t share sensitive personal details – Avoid posting your address, phone number, or passwords.
Don’t engage in cyberbullying or trolling – Respect others’ opinions and boundaries.
Don’t overshare – Keep a balance between personal and public life.
Don’t post inappropriate content – Avoid content that could harm your reputation.
Don’t ignore digital footprints – Everything you post online can leave a lasting record.

This checklist helps promote safe, respectful, and professional behavior on social media.
